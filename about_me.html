About Me

Sean Chew

Hello! I'm an Urban Planning graduate student at Columbia University with concentrations in Urban Analytics and Built Environment. So far, I've worked with the New York City Mayor's Office of Climate and Environmental Justice as a GIS Intern, and I'll be using those skills at the Federal Transit Authority as a data scientist student trainee.

Before grad school, I was an energy analyst for The Brattle Group, worked as a power systems engineer at Eaton Corporation, designed solar systems at Hannah Solar LLC, and studied Math/Econ and Electrical engineering at Emory and Georgia Tech. My hope is to use my knowledge of urbanism and technology to improve people's lives.
